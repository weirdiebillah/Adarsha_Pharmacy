About Us

Established 25 years ago, Adarsha Pharmacy has been a pillar of healthcare in the Kalshi community. Founded by Md. Liaquate Hossain, we continue his legacy of providing trusted treatments and exceptional service.

Our mission is to ensure access to high-quality, affordable medicines and healthcare services for all members of our community.
